What in the web...

Everything has a past. Everything has a future. Things are constantly changing, evolving.. growing. The world of web is no different. In a world full of technology, will we ever stop reinventing? If you take a look at the history of the web, it isn’t hard to tell what the future holds within it. Think of how you would use the computer only just 30 years ago versus now. Developers are continuously creating bigger, bolder and better ways for us to interact with our computers. I think that there will never be an end to the web. There will never be a day where we have seen it all and reached a dead end. To me that’s the beauty of it. We’ve come to a day in age where technology is becoming a foundation of the human existence.Look around. I bet you can’t make a full circle without seeing something that has to do with software development of some form. At this point, we have to keep updating and creating.
